All FAQs
Is there a discount with the Munich Card or the Munich City Pass?

Yes, with the Munich Card, you get the following discounts at our museum ticket desk:

Monday to Friday (excluding during and on holidays): 20% discount on museum admission.

Saturday and Sunday (as well as during and on holidays): 10% discount on museum admission.

Attention: The discount is only valid on site – it cannot be redeemed in the online ticket shop and it is only valid for individual tickets and is not applicable to group bookings or anything else.

Are there discounts for people with disabilities and their accompanying persons?

Yes, we offer discounted tickets for people with disabilities. Accompanying persons receive free admission if there is a “B” in the severely disabled pass.

Are cultural or social passports also recognized?

We are constantly working on new collaborations and will inform you about news via our website and our social media channels.

Are there any special promotions or time-limited discounts?

Yes, we always have special discount campaigns, which we will inform you about on our website and on social media.

Is there a family discount?

Yes, we offer discounted family tickets for 2 adults and 2 children. Additional children can be booked on top individually – also at a discounted price.
